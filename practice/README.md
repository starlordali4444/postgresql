## 🗓️ Weekly Drill Schedule

### Week 1: The Basics (100 Questions)

- 30x `SELECT` & `WHERE`
- 20x `ORDER BY` & `LIMIT`
- 20x String Functions (`UPPER`, `LOWER`, `SUBSTRING`)
- 30x Logical Operators (`AND`, `OR`, `IN`, `BETWEEN`)

### Week 2: Summarizing Data (80 Questions)

- 40x `GROUP BY` & Aggregates
- 20x `HAVING` clause filters
- 20x Date Aggregations (Monthly, Yearly trends)

### Week 3: Connecting Tables (80 Questions)

- 30x Inner Joins (2 Tables)
- 30x Left/Right Joins
- 20x Complex Multi-Table Joins (3+ Tables)

### Week 4 & 5: Advanced Techniques (90 Questions)

- 30x Subqueries (Scalar & Correlated)
- 30x CTEs (Common Table Expressions)
- 30x Window Functions (`RANK`, `LEAD`, `LAG`)

---

## 🎯 Pro Tips for Success

1.  **Don't Rush**: It's better to solve 1 question deeply than 10 superficially.
2.  **Draw It Out**: Visualize table connections on paper before typing `JOIN`.
3.  **Read the Errors**: SQL errors tell you exactly what's wrong. Read them!
4.  **Google Smartly**: Search for "PostgreSQL string functions" not "how to solve question 4".
